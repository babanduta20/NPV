FOUNDING PHILOSOPHY

- The National People's Voice Party (NPV) is a people-first party, prioritizing the voices of citizens in governance. It promotes inclusive leadership, participatory democracy, sustainable growth, self-reliance, economic transformation, the rule of law, and national unity as enshrined in the Constitution of Kenya.
- NPV's philosophy emphasizes genuine representation of the people, strengthening governance institutions, upholding the separation of powers, and fostering democratic representation, as detailed in the party manifesto.
